Personality Profile

Myers-Briggs Test

According to the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator test, I am an ENFJ-T Protagonist personality. People with
this personality type are typically strong leaders full of passion and charisma. They makeup 2% of the
general population (Introduction | Protagonist ENFJ 2020) and often take up roles in politics, teaching,
or coaching, as they are very influential people. They take great pride in leading and guiding others to
work together to make themselves and the world a better place. Protagonists can use their idealism and
vision to overcome challenges that they may face, often resulting in improving the lives of those around
them while they do this. However as helpful as they can be, often this can lead to them being taken advantage
of by other personality types looking to exploit their good nature. This does not deter them from being an
outstanding individual dedicated to inspiring and helping those around them.
